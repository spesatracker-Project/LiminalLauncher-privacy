Privacy Policy

Liminimal · Last updated 21 July 2026

The short version. Liminimal is a minimalist launcher built to help you use your phone less. It is designed to collect as little as possible about you.

  • We run no servers and receive no data from the app. There is no account to create and nothing is sent to the developer.
  • No advertising, no analytics, no trackers, no crash-reporting.
  • Almost everything stays on your device. Data leaves your phone in only three cases, each under your control: weather, optional backup, and optional web search.

When data leaves your device

There are exactly three cases, and you decide whether they happen.

1. Weather (OpenMeteo)

If you use the weather widget or weather theme, the app sends an approximate location (or a city name you choose) to the weather service OpenMeteo to get the forecast for your area. The coordinates are sent only to retrieve the weather; we do not store them, and nothing else is sent. If you deny the Location permission, you can still pick your city by hand.

OpenMeteo's privacy policy: https://open-meteo.com/en/terms

2. Optional backup (Google Drive)

If — and only if — you choose to back up your setup, the app saves your launcher settings (slots, folders, widgets, limits and appearance preferences) to your own Google Drive, in a hidden folder reserved for the app (appDataFolder).

  • The backup lives in your Google account, not ours. We never see it.
  • It is stored in a private area that other apps and people cannot browse.
  • Signing in uses Google Sign-In; we request only the minimum scope needed to read and write that one hidden folder (drive.appdata).
  • You can delete the backup at any time by disconnecting the account in the app, or from your Google account's data controls.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Web search (DuckDuckGo)

When you use quick search and choose to search the web, your typed query is opened in your browser as a DuckDuckGo search. This only happens when you explicitly ask to search the web. If you just search apps and contacts, nothing is sent anywhere.

DuckDuckGo's privacy policy: https://duckduckgo.com/privacy

What we never do

  • We never sell or share your data for advertising.
  • We show no ads and embed no advertising SDKs.
  • We use no analytics, tracking, profiling, or crash-reporting tools.
  • We have no user accounts and no server that stores your data.

Keeping and deleting your data

Because your data lives on your device:

  • Uninstalling the app removes the settings stored on your phone.
  • To remove a Google Drive backup, disconnect the account in the app or delete the app's data from your Google account.
  • App usage data and contacts are provided by Android and are not copied anywhere by us; revoking the relevant permission stops the app from reading them.

Children

Liminimal is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any data about them. Since the app collects nothing on our side, it holds no such data.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the EU/UK GDPR), you have rights over your personal data, including access, correction, and deletion. Because we hold no data about you on any server, most of these rights you exercise directly on your device (through Android's permission and app settings) and, for backups, through your own Google account. If you have any question, contact us at the address above.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, for significant changes, note them in the app's release notes.
